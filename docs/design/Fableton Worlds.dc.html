CONTEXT & DESIGN REASONING
Grounded in the repo's charter template + architecture docs. Each world is a living diorama — same engine chrome (clock, chronicle ticker, inspect panel, time control), different charter skin, proving the divergence acceptance test visually. UI is hybrid: quiet dark-glass chrome; diegetic, charter-styled panels. Legibility is discoverable: behavior-tree labels appear when you click a resident; the studio surfaces as a construction site you can click (1a). Scenes tick at ~2.6s like the real world-sim (slow deltas, client interpolation). Try: drag to orbit · click a resident · change the hour · in 1a, click the sign in the dirt.
